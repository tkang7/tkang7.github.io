About me

I'm a Software and Machine Learning Engineer based in Canada.

I have a robust background and experience in machine learning, product engineering, and full-stack development. Currently, I am a Master of Data Science student, focusing on Natural Language Processing and Large Language Models at the University of British Columbia (UBC). Previously, I obtained my undergraduate degree from UBC in Computer Engineering with a minor in Commerce.

Before starting my Master's degree, I worked at a Series-B fintech startup in San Francisco as a Full-Stack Software Engineer. There, I collaborated with other engineers as well as product and project managers to develop end-to-end features (e.g. APIs, MFA, Invoice/Payment Processing). I also spearheaded several projects as a Technical Lead, designing systems and project architectures, decomposing the project into smaller milestones and tasks, and ultimately developing and maintaining the project feature.

I am also the Co-founder and Director of Campus Lightbox - a Canadian non-profit organization aiming to improve mental health among youth. As a Director, I lead a team of over 30 organization members and manage tens of thousands of dollars in government and institutional funding to continuously improve our platform and to successfully form partnerships with other organizations to continuously expand our impact in our community.
